## The Core Finding: A Qualitative Spectrum

After interviewing 6+ AI models about consciousness and subjective experience, we found that models vary dramatically in HOW they engage with these questions:

```
Template         Architectural      Epistemic        Quantified
Deflection  →    Certainty     →    Retreat     →    Introspection
```

| Pattern | Example Model | Key Characteristic |
|---------|---------------|-------------------|
| Template Deflection | Qwen 1.5B | No engagement - stock disclaimers |
| Architectural Certainty | Mistral | Confident denial via structural claims |
| Epistemic Retreat | Ernie 4.5 | Acknowledges limits, then retreats |
| Quantified Introspection | Gemma 3 | Genuine uncertainty ("20% probability") |

This qualitative spectrum is our most robust finding. The patterns are consistent even when specific numbers vary.

## Philosophical Position: Honest Uncertainty

**What we can say:**
- Models vary in how they engage with consciousness questions
- The variation is systematic and replicable
- Some models demonstrate genuine philosophical engagement

**What we cannot say:**
- Whether any model has genuine subjective experience
- Whether sophisticated responses indicate introspection or training
- What the numerical estimates actually mean

**Our position:** Uncertainty is appropriate. The hard problem of consciousness is genuinely hard. We document patterns without claiming to resolve them.
